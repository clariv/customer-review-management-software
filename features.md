# Features

## Review Management

Manage customer reviews from different platforms in one dashboard.

## AI Sentiment Analysis

Analyze customer emotions and classify reviews as positive, neutral, negative, or mixed.

## Unified Inbox

Reply to reviews, customer messages, and brand mentions from one place.

## Analytics Dashboard

Track ratings, review trends, sentiment score, response time, and location performance.

## Multi-Location Management

Manage reviews and reputation for multiple business locations.

## Social Media Monitoring

Monitor customer conversations, comments, and mentions across social platforms.
